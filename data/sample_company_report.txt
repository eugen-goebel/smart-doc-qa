NovaTech Solutions GmbH — Annual Report 2025
=============================================

1. Company Overview
-------------------
NovaTech Solutions GmbH is a German technology company founded in 2018 and
headquartered in Munich, Bavaria. The company specializes in enterprise
software solutions for manufacturing and logistics, with a focus on AI-powered
process optimization. As of December 2025, NovaTech employs 342 people across
three offices in Munich, Berlin, and Hamburg.

The company's core product is "OptiFlow," a cloud-based platform that uses
machine learning to optimize supply chain operations. OptiFlow serves over
180 enterprise customers in 12 countries, primarily in the DACH region
(Germany, Austria, Switzerland) and Scandinavia.

2. Financial Performance
-------------------------
NovaTech achieved significant growth in fiscal year 2025:

  - Total Revenue: EUR 47.3 million (up 31% from EUR 36.1 million in 2024)
  - Recurring Revenue (SaaS): EUR 38.2 million (81% of total revenue)
  - Gross Margin: 72.4% (up from 68.9% in 2024)
  - EBITDA: EUR 8.1 million (17.1% margin, first full year of profitability)
  - Net Income: EUR 3.4 million (compared to a loss of EUR 1.2 million in 2024)
  - Cash Position: EUR 22.7 million (no debt)

Revenue growth was driven by strong expansion in the Scandinavian market (+58%)
and successful upselling of the new "OptiFlow Analytics" module to existing
customers. The average contract value increased by 22% to EUR 212,000 per year.

3. Product & Technology
------------------------
OptiFlow Platform Modules:
  - OptiFlow Core: Real-time supply chain monitoring and alerting
  - OptiFlow Analytics: AI-powered demand forecasting and inventory optimization
  - OptiFlow Connect: API gateway for ERP and WMS integrations (SAP, Oracle, Microsoft)
  - OptiFlow Green: Carbon footprint tracking and sustainability reporting (launched Q3 2025)

Technology Stack:
  - Backend: Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL
  - ML Pipeline: PyTorch, MLflow, Kubeflow
  - Infrastructure: AWS (primary), Azure (for Microsoft-integrated customers)
  - Frontend: React, TypeScript

Key technology milestones in 2025:
  - Launched OptiFlow Green module for ESG compliance reporting
  - Reduced ML model inference latency by 40% through model distillation
  - Achieved SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications
  - Released public API v3 with GraphQL support

4. Market & Competition
------------------------
NovaTech operates in the supply chain management (SCM) software market, which
is projected to grow from USD 19.3 billion in 2024 to USD 37.4 billion by 2030
(CAGR of 11.7%, according to MarketsAndMarkets).

Key Competitors:
  - Kinaxis (Canada): Market leader in concurrent planning; larger but less AI-focused
  - o9 Solutions (USA): Strong in digital brain platform; aggressive growth strategy
  - Blue Yonder (USA): Acquired by Panasonic; strong in retail but less agile
  - INFORM GmbH (Germany): Regional competitor; strong in optimization algorithms

NovaTech's competitive advantages:
  1. Deep DACH market expertise and German data compliance (GDPR-native architecture)
  2. Superior AI/ML capabilities compared to legacy competitors
  3. Strong integration with SAP (critical for German Mittelstand customers)
  4. Carbon tracking module addresses growing ESG requirements

8. Risks & Challenges
----------------------
  - Talent competition: Hiring ML engineers in Germany remains difficult
  - Enterprise sales cycles: Average 6-9 months; longer in pharma (12+ months)
  - Currency risk: Growing Scandinavian revenue is exposed to SEK/NOK fluctuations
  - Technology risk: Rapid evolution of LLMs could disrupt existing ML approaches
  - Concentration risk: Top 10 customers represent 41% of recurring revenue
  - Regulatory: EU AI Act compliance requires ongoing investment
